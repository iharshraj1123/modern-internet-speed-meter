# Privacy Policy for Internet Speed Meter

**Last Updated:** July 21, 2026

**Internet Speed Meter** ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the Application on Windows.

---

## 1. Overview: 100% Offline & Private
Internet Speed Meter is designed from the ground up to protect your privacy. **All bandwidth monitoring, process telemetry, and traffic statistics are calculated locally on your personal device and stored exclusively in a local database (`speed_meter.db`).** 

We do not collect, transmit, share, or sell any of your personal or network data to any remote server or third party.

---

## 2. Information Handled Locally

The Application measures system network interface activity to display real-time network speeds and historical data usage:

* **Network Usage Data:** Real-time upload and download speeds, aggregated hourly, daily, and monthly bandwidth volume.
* **Process Telemetry:** Network traffic generated by active applications running on your device (e.g., process name, bytes sent/received).
* **Application Settings:** Your personal preferences (such as theme choice, widget opacity, accent color, position, and autostart preferences).

**Where it is stored:** All data is stored locally in your Windows user profile folder (`AppData/Local/Internet Speed Meter/speed_meter.db`).

---

## 3. Data Collection and Transmission

* **No Server Uploads:** The Application does **not** connect to external servers or send telemetry data to us or any third party.
* **No User Accounts:** You do not need an account, email address, or login to use the Application.
* **No Analytics / Ad Trackers:** The Application contains no advertising SDKs, third-party analytics scripts, or tracking services.

---

## 4. Optional Third-Party Services & Links

The Application contains optional links to external platforms:
* **Support / Donation Links:** Optional links to external platforms (such as Ko-fi) to support development. Clicking these links opens your default system web browser. Any interaction on external websites is governed by the respective platform's privacy policy.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

* **Auto-Purge & Compaction:** The Application automatically prunes detailed 5-minute raw telemetry older than 7 days and hourly rollups older than 90 days to keep the local database lightweight.
* **Manual Reset:** You can clear all recorded history at any time directly in the Application under **Settings -> Storage Analytics -> Clear Telemetry History**.
* **Complete Removal:** Uninstalling the Application deletes its local files and data from your device.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy
The Application does not collect or solicit personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
